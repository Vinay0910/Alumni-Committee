Alumni Awards
2020-2021

‘Distinguished Alumni’ award under the Category of ‘CRCE Young Achiever’. Mr. Manthan Dakshini,

In presenting you with this award we agree with the dozens of people nominating you, that success and talent like yours needs to not only be recognized, but also showcased to our entire community.We hope that all the students of Fr. CRCE draw from your story of not only being excellent at academics but also successfully translating those skills into creating and operating a successful business venture

‘Distinguished Alumni’ under the Category ‘CRCE Corporate Leader’. Mr. Anup Purohit

In presenting you with this award we agree with the dozens of people nominating you, that success and talent like yours needs to not only be recognized, but also showcased to our entire community. Through your work you have indeed shown us that an engineer can excel at anything he puts his mind to. Your achievements through the years including the plethora of multinational companies you have been associated with, are the envy of any young graduate and we hope you will be a role model to these young engineers

‘Distinguished Alumni’ under the Category ‘CRCE Social Impact Leader’.

Mr. Nandan Bhat

Through your work you have shown us that engineering is indeed a human field and that done right, it can lead to lasting positive change. The growth of your company is proof that engineers can lead the social and environmental revolution through innovation and efficiency. We are indeed blessed to count you among our alumni family and we will continue to support you in all your future ventures.

‘Distinguished Alumni’ under the Category ‘CRCE Entrepreneurial Leader’.

Mr. Mihir Karkare

Your work over the years since graduation is testament to the fact that hard work can make dreams come true, and we are sure that the hundreds of students who have heard your story have gone back with their minds swarming with ideas of their own entrepreneurial ventures. We respect and value your contribution to society and your impact on our young engineers and we are proud to have you as a part of the CRCE alumni family.

‘Distinguished Alumni’ under the Category ‘CRCE Top Entertainer’. Mr. Chetan Shashital

Your excellent work in the entertainment industry is a beacon of hope for our talented youngsters who wish to spread out into the world to explore and hone their talents in hopes of one day becoming a star entertainer. We are immensely thankful to you for showing the world that CRCE is indeed blessed with the most talented students and we are extremely happy to count you among the CRCE Alumni family.
